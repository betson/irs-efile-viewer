Translate Raw Data Back Into a Form

The IRS has released over a million electronic 990 filings (nonprofit annual tax filings) as XML documents. Accessible on Amazon Web Services, these filings are perfect for researchers, developers, and data scientists. But sometimes it helps to turn the data back into what it came from: a paper form.

Stylesheets and code are availabe open-source on GitHub.
